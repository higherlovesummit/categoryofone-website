Mirasee

A scalable house of brands for a company growing by acquisition.

Unified
A Fragmented Portfolio Made Whole

The Challenge

Mirasee, a successful business education company, was on a scale trajectory - acquiring multiple companies and accumulating sub-brands, products, and programs. The fragmented brand architecture threatened its market clarity.

The Transformation

We created a powerful strategy that organized Mirasee's brand architecture into a scalable "house of brands" - then executed a complete brand evolution with a new visual identity and brand guidelines.

The Result

Every sub-entity now lives under one unified identity, solidifying Mirasee's leadership in education and readying it for its next phase of growth.
